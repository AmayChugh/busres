About Us

Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - Trip- Tours and Travels is a pioneer in India’s online travel industry.

Founded in the year 2021 by a group of friends- Shubham Khandelwal,Amay Chugh, Anushka Rajoriya, Reeti Kothari and Sehla Pathan. SafarKaro came to life to empower the Indian traveller with instant bookings and comprehensive choices. The company initiated its journey serving the Pune-Mumbai-Banglore-Gwalior travel market offering a range of best-value products and services powered by technology and round-the-clock customer support. After consolidating its position in the market as a brand recognised for its reliability and transparency, Trip- Tours and Travels launched its Pune operations in 2005. With more and more Punekars initiating to transact online with ICRTC and new opportunities with the advent of low cost carriers, SafarKaro offered travellers the convenience of booking travel online with a few clicks. SafarKaro’s rise has been led by the vision and the spirit of each one of its employees, for whom no idea was too big and no problem too difficult. With untiring determination, SafarKaro has proactively diversified its product offering, adding a variety of online and offline products and services.
